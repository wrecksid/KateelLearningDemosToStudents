# Attribution & Usage Policy

This repository is intended for teaching, learning, extension, and experimentation.
If you reuse, adapt, extend, or build on any code or materials here, the three obligations
below are **mandatory** — not optional.

---

## ⚠️ Mandatory Requirements for ALL Users

These requirements apply to **faculty, students, researchers, and any other individual or organisation**
that uses, adapts, or builds on material from this repository.

### 1. Credit in Every Presentation

You **must** include the following acknowledgement slide or statement in **every presentation,
lecture, workshop, webinar, demo, or public talk** in which this material — or any derivative
of it — appears:

> *"Demos and course material adapted from the* ***KateelLearningDemosToStudents*** *repository
> by Professor Vinaya Sathyanarayana (GitHub: VinayaSharada).*
> *Repository: https://github.com/VinayaSharada/KateelLearningDemosToStudents"*

This credit must appear in:
- presentation slides (title slide or a dedicated acknowledgements slide)
- lecture notes and handouts
- lab sheets and assignment briefs
- course repositories and syllabi
- recorded video descriptions or transcripts
- any published reports or notebooks derived from this work

### 2. Star the Repository on GitHub ⭐

You **must** star the repository at
**https://github.com/VinayaSharada/KateelLearningDemosToStudents**
before or at the point of first use.
This is a lightweight but meaningful signal of acknowledgement to the author.

### 3. Mandatory Usage Notification Email 📧

You **must** send an email to **vinallcontact@gmail.com** with the subject line:

```
[KateelLearningDemos] Usage Notification — <Your Name / Institution>
```

Include in the body:
- Your name and role (faculty / student / researcher / practitioner / other)
- Your institution or organisation
- The course name, event, or project in which the material is being used
- Approximate start date of use
- A link to your course page, slide deck, or project (if publicly available)

This notification is **required** the first time you use the material and whenever you
begin a new course, cohort, or project using it.

> **Why is this mandatory?**
> It lets the author track educational impact, reach out with updates, and
> acknowledge the community of users building on this work.

---

## Faculty Use

Faculty members who use this repository in their course material should acknowledge and
attribute Professor Vinaya Sathyanarayana in all of the above, plus:

- course syllabi and learning-management-system pages
- any published academic papers or conference proceedings that rely on these demos

## Student and Builder Use

Students and other builders who extend or adapt the material must provide attribution in:

- project reports and theses
- presentation slides and demos
- Jupyter notebooks and code repositories
- any public-facing project documentation

## Suggested Attribution Line

```
Adapted from material in KateelLearningDemosToStudents by Professor Vinaya Sathyanarayana.
https://github.com/VinayaSharada/KateelLearningDemosToStudents
```

## Register Your Usage

Once you have sent your notification email, please also self-register in the
**[Usage Registry Wiki](https://github.com/VinayaSharada/KateelLearningDemosToStudents/wiki/Usage-Registry)**
so the broader community can see how this material is being used worldwide.

## Notes

- Attribution must be retained even when the material is substantially modified.
- Attribution is required for both direct reuse and clearly derivative work.
- Failure to comply with the mandatory requirements above is a violation of the spirit
  of open educational resources and academic integrity norms.
