# Supply Chain Finance

Demo for supply chain financing and trade credit optimization.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand supply chain finance mechanisms
- Learn about dynamic discounting
- Explore buyer-seller financing
- Understand risk-return tradeoffs

## How to Run

1. Open `index.html` in browser
2. Input supply chain parameters
3. See financing options and benefits
4. Explore cash flow optimization

## Key Concepts Demonstrated

- **Dynamic Discounting**: Early payment at discounted rates
- **Supply Chain Finance**: Working capital optimization
- **Risk Transfer**: Bank financing of suppliers
- **Cash Flow Management**: DSO optimization
- **Supplier Relations**: Win-win financing
