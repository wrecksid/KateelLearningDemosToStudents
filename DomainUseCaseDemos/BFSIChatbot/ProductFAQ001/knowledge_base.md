## Savings Account Minimum Balance
The regular savings account requires customers to maintain the published monthly average balance. If the balance condition is not met, the bank may apply a service charge as per the account schedule.

## Credit Card Annual Fee Waiver
Many credit cards waive the annual fee when the customer crosses the eligible annual spend threshold. The exact waiver condition depends on the card variant and should be checked in the product schedule.

## Personal Loan Prepayment
Personal loans may allow partial or full prepayment after the lock-in period. Charges can depend on whether the rate is fixed or floating and whether the borrower is an individual or business customer.

## Insurance Claim Document Checklist
Motor and health insurance claims usually require the policy number, claim form, identity proof, and supporting incident or medical documents. Missing documents can delay claim processing.

## UPI Transaction Dispute
If a UPI transaction fails or is disputed, customers should first verify whether the amount was debited, then raise a complaint through the app or bank support channel with the transaction reference number.
