## 🎯 Overview

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) is a critical metric that predicts the total value a customer will bring to a business throughout their relationship. For credit card companies, CLV analysis helps in:

- **Customer Acquisition**: Determining how much to spend on acquiring new customers
- **Customer Retention**: Identifying high-value customers at risk of churning
- **Product Development**: Creating targeted products for different customer segments
- **Marketing Strategy**: Optimizing marketing spend based on customer value
- **Risk Management**: Balancing profitability with risk exposure

This project demonstrates various CLV calculation methods, customer segmentation techniques, and provides actionable insights for bank management.
