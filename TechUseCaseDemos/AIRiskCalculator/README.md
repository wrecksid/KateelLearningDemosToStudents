# AI Risk Calculator

## Learning Objectives
- Understand key risk factors in AI projects
- Learn to assess technical, operational, and compliance risks
- Practice quantitative risk assessment techniques

## How to Run
1. Open `index.html` in a browser
2. Adjust sliders to model different risk scenarios
3. View real-time risk assessment

## Risk Factors
- **Model Complexity**: Higher complexity = higher technical risk
- **Data Quality**: Poor data = higher technical risk
- **Regulatory Pressure**: More regulation = higher compliance risk
- **Business Impact**: Higher impact = higher operational/compliance risk
