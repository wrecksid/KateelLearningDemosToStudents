🎓 PM Reflection — Decision Framing
🎯 Decision Should we build, buy, use an API, or partner for this AI capability? This is one of the most recurring sourcing decisions in AI product management.
📊 Assumed Slider inputs are self-assessed estimates. Real decisions require vendor POCs, legal data-sharing review, formal build estimates, and total cost of ownership modelling.
⚠️ Risk This model treats all constraints as independent linear inputs. Real decisions have complex interdependencies — e.g. high privacy AND high customisation together strongly favour Build, not just each individually.
💡 PM Override A PM might choose API despite high privacy concerns if a signed DPA and a private deployment option exist with the vendor. Scoring models inform — they don't decide.
