Decision: How to price an AI product without destroying margin at scale.
📊
Assumed: Costs are fixed-rate per token. Real inference costs vary by model, provider, and batching efficiency. Volume discounts and reserved capacity can cut LLM costs 30–60%.
⚠️
Risk: Heavy users destroy unit economics. This model uses a 4× multiplier — real power users can be 20–50× average. The distribution matters more than the mean.
💡
Override: A strong PM would validate heavy-user behavior from actual usage logs before setting pricing tiers. Never ship a pricing model based purely on spreadsheet assumptions.
